Why it works smoothly.

You don't need to know any of this. But if you're curious:

Apps can't break your system

Each app runs in its own protected space. If one app has a problem, it can't affect anything else.

Updates can be undone

If an update causes a problem, you can go back to how things were before. One click.

No viruses

The way BoringOS is built makes it extremely resistant to viruses and malware. You don't need antivirus software.

Stays fast

Unlike Windows, BoringOS doesn't slow down over time. It stays fast because the system stays clean.
